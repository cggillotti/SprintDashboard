CMS-3144
Update designs for Short Bio (Cascade)
SP: - Assignee: Cody Koscielski In Status: 6d
Imported: Design Considerations Short Bios are used when authors don’t want/need to make a dedicated Bio page for someone. They display a group of people in a succinct manner. They should not be styled like Bio Listings, because those are clickable cards. Since Short Bios don’t link out anywhere, it would be confusing/frustrating to users if they tried to click on them. How might we share details about our team members on the same page, so we can reduce the number of times a user has to click through?
